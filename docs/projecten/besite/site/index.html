About BeSite

BeSite is de internetspecialist uit de Achterhoek die bedrijven helpt naar meer online resultaat. Met een team van specialisten werken ze dagelijks aan internetprojecten voor diverse mooie opdrachtgevers. BeSite denkt graag met je mee. Samen sparren om een passende online strategie vast te stellen. Korte communicatielijnen en afspraak is afspraak kenmerken onze werkwijze.

Projects

The exact assignment has not been created yet. At the moment of writing this text, we know we will be providing security advice for BeSite. We will most likely be assessing the security situation for a few customers. Right now, security is important for BeSite, but is not of high priority. We will use various pen testing techniques to properly assess the current security. A few examples of customers we will be asssesing are: The parking system for Eindhoven Airport, the website for Amsterdam RAI and Amsterdam Arena, and the ticket system for Mojo Concerts. We're also going to assess the companies for Smartness. We will provide information about Smart Industry where possible, and let the companies know what processes could be changed to achieve a higher level of Smartness.
